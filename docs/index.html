MuseMood is designed so the quietest layer of the product is also the most trustworthy one.

A more editorial trust surface

Instead of a generic legal wall, the privacy experience is framed like a museum companion: warm materials, deliberate pacing, precise summaries, and a direct route into the full text.

  • The landing page acts as a trust preface rather than a throwaway redirect.
  • The policy page is broken into readable, navigable sections with quick-scan summaries.
  • The visual system uses the MuseMood mark and a restrained gallery-inspired palette.
Premium, not ornamental

The design avoids stock gradients and template-looking UI, while staying readable and deploy-safe on static hosting.

What the app does not do

MuseMood does not ask for an account, does not embed ad networks, and does not build a behavioral profile around your taste.

What the full policy covers

Device storage, cache behavior, network requests to public museum sources, third-party links, deletion paths, non-affiliation language, and direct contact details are all documented in the main policy page.
