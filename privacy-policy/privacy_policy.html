Privacy Policy

MuseMood is a local-first museum art-card curation app. This policy explains what the app stores, what it does not collect, and how public museum source data is used.

1. App identity

MuseMood helps users discover, browse, and curate public museum artwork cards through a mood-based, collectible art-card experience.

MuseMood is designed as a local-first app. It does not require an account, login, or user profile to use the core experience.

2. No account

MuseMood does not require account creation, login, profile registration, or user authentication.

MuseMood does not ask you to provide your name, email address, password, phone number, or profile information inside the app.

3. No ads, analytics, or tracking

MuseMood does not intentionally include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, or behavioral profiling tools.

MuseMood does not sell user data.

This privacy policy page is also a static page. It does not use cookies, analytics scripts, advertising scripts, or external tracking pixels.

4. Local data on your device

MuseMood may store app data locally on your device to provide the art-card experience and improve offline readiness.

  • Saved and favorite artwork cards
  • Selected mood and source preferences
  • Theme, reduced motion, haptics, and app settings
  • Cached artwork images
  • Cached public museum metadata
  • Prepared daily or session decks
  • Local session and readiness state for startup and playability
  • Source health and readiness metadata

Local-first note: this data is intended to remain on your device unless you clear app data, clear cache, uninstall the app, or use available in-app cache controls.

5. Network requests

MuseMood connects to public museum APIs and public knowledge APIs to load public artwork records, artwork images, and source metadata.

These sources may include:

  • The Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Rijksmuseum
  • Wellcome Collection
  • Wikidata

MuseMood may also load artwork images from museum image servers, IIIF servers, CDN image URLs, or other image URLs returned by those public source APIs.

These network requests are used to provide app functionality. Third-party museums, public source APIs, image servers, or websites may receive standard technical request information, such as your IP address and request metadata, according to their own privacy practices.

6. External links

When you tap a source record link, MuseMood may open an official museum or source record webpage in your browser or another app.

Those external websites are not operated by MuseMood and may follow their own privacy policies and terms.

7. Data deletion

You can remove app data from your device through Android system settings by clearing app data or storage.

MuseMood also provides an in-app image cache clear control. Clearing the image cache is intended to remove cached artwork images, not your saved private gallery items.

Uninstalling the app may also remove locally stored app data, depending on your Android device and system settings.

8. Children

MuseMood is not designed specifically for children and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

9. Source attribution and non-affiliation

Artwork records, images, metadata, and source pages belong to their respective institutions and public source providers.

MuseMood is not affiliated with, sponsored by, or endorsed by The Metropolitan Museum of Art, Rijksmuseum, Wellcome Collection, Wikidata, or any other source provider.

Artwork metadata may appear in the original language or format provided by the museum or public source. MuseMood does not automatically translate artwork titles, artist names, source records, or museum-provided metadata.
